UNiDAYS Competency and Progression Framework

üîÆ Usage

This framework outlines examples of desirable behaviours and skills, arranged into different high level competencies, finer grained topics and individual levels. It is a tool that helps employees and managers:

  • make development and career plans
  • talk about what we're looking for from each role in a consistent way
  • set a fair level of compensation.

The framework is a compass, not a GPS.

It's meant to be helpful. It's not meant to be a rating system for humans, free from edge cases.

üôã How does it work?

The framework covers all the things we're looking for from each role at UNiDAYS. We have a suite of core-competencies and then more specific ones for each role.

Each competency topic is sorted into levels and we try to give specific examples of behaviours we expect for each.

Your manager will work with you on this. None of it will happen mysteriously behind closed doors. You'll agree what level of progression you're going for and what you need to improve on with your manager. It should be clear how you're doing relative to that at all times.
